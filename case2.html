Farm to Table

description

North America is moving towards Veganism or Vegetarianism due to the known environmental impacts of red meat. By shifting to a more vegetable-based diet, one person can save approximately 219,000 gallons of water a year. In addition to consuming colossal amounts of water annually, the meat industry contributes to the emission of greenhouse gases, deforestation, water pollution, and waste of food resources â€” all of which hasten the effects of global warming. Our project intends to emphasize the environmental impact of producing 1lb of beef by outlining its production process from farm to table.
